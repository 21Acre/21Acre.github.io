About us

  • 21Acre farm (Agriculture)

    • Our farm is a vibrant ecosystem

    where pigs, fish, and cows thrive in sustainable environments designed to optimize their well-being and productivity. Through careful stewardship, we prioritize ethical animal husbandry practices, ensuring the highest standards of welfare. Furthermore, our commitment to organic materials and farming methods underscores our dedication to environmental sustainability and the production of wholesome, nutritious food. By harnessing the power of nature and embracing organic principles, we strive to cultivate healthier ecosystems and communities. Join us as we sow the seeds of change and cultivate a future where agriculture nourishes both the body and the planet..

  • 21Acre media(Arts)

    • In our vibrant arts community,

    we celebrate diversity and self-expression through a myriad of mediums, from captivating singing performances to electrifying rap battles and mesmerizing modeling showcases. One of our standout events is the MS Organic Pageant, a unique beauty competition that challenges contestants to redefine fashion by creating stunning ensembles using only organic materials. This innovative platform not only showcases the beauty of sustainable fashion but also inspires creativity and environmental consciousness. At 21acre, we believe in the transformative power of the arts to ignite dialogue, inspire change, and foster connection. Join us as we amplify voices, cultivate talent, and unleash the boundless potential of artistic expression.

  • 21Acre farmmacy(health care)

    • At our farmmacy

    we offer a unique selection of organic medicines crafted with care from nature's bounty. Rooted in the principles of natural healing, our remedies harness the power of organic ingredients to promote health and vitality while minimizing harm to the planet. Our approach to healthcare extends beyond traditional medicine, as we prioritize practices that support environmental sustainability and ecosystem health. From eco-friendly packaging to responsible sourcing, every aspect of our operations reflects our dedication to preserving the natural world. Join us as we embark on a journey toward a healthier future, where people and the environment thrive in harmony. Together, we can cultivate wellness for generations to come.
